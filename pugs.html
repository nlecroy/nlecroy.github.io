All About Pugs

Learn about these adorable wrinkly dogs!

Cool Pug Facts

  • Pugs originated in China over 2,000 years ago
  • They were bred to be companions for Chinese emperors
  • A group of pugs is called a "grumble"
  • Pugs are known for their wrinkly faces and curly tails

Why Pugs Are Awesome

Pugs are playful, loving, and make great family pets. They love to cuddle and are great with kids!
